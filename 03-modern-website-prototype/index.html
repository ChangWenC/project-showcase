Learn From the Past

All artists, human or machine, are influenced by what came before. From Renaissance masters to modern creatives, every great work builds upon history. Echoes of the Masters reveals those lineages through AI-driven visual analysis.

Discover the origins of your art

Upload an artwork and explore its stylistic ancestry. See how brushwork, composition, and color link your image to the great artists of the past.

Understand the Ethics

Echoes is grounded in Kantian deontology. It challenges the idea that AI is ethically inferior in learning. If it's acceptable for humans to learn from art history, then the same must apply to AI—if we hold moral rules to be universal.
